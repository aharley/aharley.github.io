Adam Harley

I am joining Prof. Leonidas Guibas' lab at Stanford University, as a postdoc.

I recently completed my Ph.D. at Carnegie Mellon University, in The Robotics Institute. I worked with Dr. Katerina Fragkiadaki, on machine learning and computer vision. Before this, I did a Master of Science in computer science, with Dr. Kosta Derpanis. Even earlier, I did a Bachelor of Arts in psychology.

I recently co-organized Perception Through Structured Generative Models at ECCV 2020, and Minds vs. Machines: How far are we from the common sense of a toddler? at CVPR 2020.
